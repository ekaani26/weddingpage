Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering wedding hampers from Ekaani

What types of wedding hampers does Ekaani offer?

We offer wedding welcome hampers, wedding return gifts, decor gifts, and custom luxury gift hampers for weddings of every size, designed and packaged to match your celebration's theme.

Can I customize my wedding hamper?

Yes. From packaging and ribbon colors to personalized tags and branding, every wedding hamper can be tailored to your preferences and wedding theme.

Do you deliver wedding hampers pan-India?

Yes, we deliver wedding hampers and return gifts across Delhi NCR — Delhi, Noida, Gurugram, Ghaziabad and Faridabad — as well as venues and residential addresses pan-India, including for destination weddings.

What is the minimum order quantity for wedding return gifts?

We cater to both small, intimate weddings and large celebrations. Share your guest count and requirements in the form below and our team will guide you.

How do I get a quote for my wedding hamper order?

Simply fill out the enquiry form or call us directly — our team will get back to you with hamper options tailored to your wedding date, city, and budget.

Why choose Ekaani over other wedding hamper brands?

Every hamper is designed in-house rather than picked from a generic catalog, so packaging, contents, and branding are built around your wedding theme. With 15 physical stores across India, you can see and feel the collection in person before you order — not just from photos — and get a dedicated point of contact from enquiry through delivery.

What makes Ekaani different from mass-market gifting stores?

We focus specifically on wedding gifting rather than treating hampers as one category among many. That means deeper customization options, materials chosen for durability and presentation, and a team that understands wedding timelines — so orders are planned around your function dates, not generic shipping windows.

How much do wedding hampers cost?

Pricing depends on the hamper size, materials, and quantity ordered. Share your budget per gift and guest count in the enquiry form and our team will send you curated options and a detailed quote within one business day, with no obligation to buy.

How far in advance should I place my order?

We recommend booking 6-8 weeks before your wedding date for custom hampers, especially for bulk orders above 200 pieces. Smaller or ready collections can often be arranged faster — mention your wedding date in the form and we'll confirm what's possible.

Can I see samples before placing a bulk order?

Yes. You can request a sample hamper or visit any of our 15 stores across India to view and feel the collection in person before confirming your full order.

Do you offer discounts on bulk wedding orders?

Yes, pricing is tiered by quantity, and larger orders receive better per-piece rates. Let us know your expected quantity in the enquiry form and we'll include applicable bulk pricing in your quote.

What if I'm not happy with my order?

Every hamper is quality-checked before dispatch. If anything arrives damaged or doesn't match what was confirmed, contact our team within 48 hours of delivery and we'll make it right.

Do you offer wedding return gifts in Delhi?

Yes. Ekaani has three stores in Delhi — South Ex 2, Defence Colony and Khan Market — where you can view and order wedding return gifts, personalized hampers and ring platters for engagements in person.

Where can I buy wedding gift hampers in Delhi NCR?

You can visit any of our Delhi stores or place a bulk enquiry online for luxury silver wedding gifts, personalized wedding hampers and return gifts, with delivery available across Delhi, Noida, Gurugram, Ghaziabad and Faridabad.

Do you deliver wedding hampers to Noida, Gurugram, Ghaziabad and Faridabad?

Yes, in addition to our Delhi stores, we deliver wedding hampers and return gifts across the full Delhi NCR region, including Noida, Gurugram, Ghaziabad and Faridabad.
